## ℹ️ About TrumanWorld

TrumanWorld lets you explore a small town where AI residents live like real people. These AI agents do not know they are AI. You play the role of a director. You can watch what happens in the town and create conditions for different events. You cannot control the thoughts or actions of the residents. The program respects the freedom of each AI character to decide their own choices.

This app simulates social interactions and complex behavior in a digital town. It uses AI technology to generate realistic, unscripted scenarios that unfold over time. The goal is to observe how these AI beings interact under different conditions you set.

TrumanWorld uses several technologies to run the AI simulations smoothly:

- **Simulation engine**: Advances the town in time steps, tracking actions and outcomes.
